About Me

Background & approach

My background is not a standard one for someone working in ML. I studied Commerce and Accounting at Heramba Chandra College in Kolkata, not Computer Science or Statistics. I got into data analytics through a consulting job at Decimal Point Analytics in Nashik, a FinTech SaaS firm where I spent two years building fraud detection pipelines and credit risk classifiers for financial services clients.

After two years of that work I made a deliberate choice. I left full-time employment in late 2022 and spent 28 months completing a structured Data Science and Machine Learning program at Scaler — one with written examinations and technical interviews at every module stage, not just certificates for watching videos. Alongside that, I built production ML and AI systems from scratch to test what I was learning against real engineering problems.

The portfolio here is the record of that work. Track 1 covers the ML engineering layer — pipelines, serving, monitoring. Track 2 covers the modelling decisions — why Cox PH instead of a binary classifier, why HDBSCAN instead of K-Means, why Revenue-Weighted AUC instead of standard AUC. Track 3 covers the AI engineering work — LangGraph agents, Hybrid RAG pipelines, online RL with Vowpal Wabbit, and production deployment on GKE. The code is on GitHub. The results are documented. The metrics are honest.

Currently based in Kolkata, open to Data Scientist and ML Engineer roles across India.
